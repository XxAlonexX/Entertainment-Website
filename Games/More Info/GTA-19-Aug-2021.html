Overview

As always, we recommend caution with all unofficial information, even from relatively reliable sources. It's true that many fans believe in the existence of GTA Remastered Trilogy in part due to Rockstar's sudden crackdown on mods to the first three 3D installments of the series. In addition, the twentieth anniversary of Grand Theft Auto III's release is approaching and such events always raise hopes for some bigger announcements from the publishers.
However, it is better to curb the enthusiasm - not once and not twice similar "celebrations" of other series have passed without any surprises. As for the leaks, we're still waiting for confirmation of those concerning Grand Theft Auto VI, because despite further rumors about the imminent announcement of the new installment nothing appeared so far.
